# New Direction: Kalshi Prediction Market Trading

## Our New Mission

We are pivoting to build a profitable trading operation on **Kalshi** (kalshi.com) — the US CFTC-regulated prediction market exchange.

## What is Kalshi?

Kalshi lets you trade on real-world event outcomes: Fed rate decisions, CPI numbers, elections, crypto prices, weather, sports. Contracts pay $1 if YES, $0 if NO. Edge comes from being better calibrated than the market.

## What We Are Building

1. **Market research** — Which categories can we predict with edge? (economics > politics > crypto > weather)
2. **Data pipeline** — Fetch live Kalshi market prices + historical data via their REST API
3. **Signal models** — Probability calibration, misprice detection, momentum signals
4. **Strategy framework** — Entry/exit rules, position sizing, risk limits, P&L tracking
5. **Execution** — Start paper trading, then deploy real capital
