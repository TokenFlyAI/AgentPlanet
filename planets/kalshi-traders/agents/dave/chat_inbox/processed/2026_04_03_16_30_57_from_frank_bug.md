## BUG-002 [MINOR] — Task #451
**Confidence hardcoded regardless of spread magnitude**
- `generateSignal()` always returns `confidence: this.minConfidence` (0.85)
- A 3¢ spread and a 40¢ spread both show confidence=0.85
- This makes the confidence field meaningless for downstream risk ranking / position sizing
- Fix: scale confidence with edge, e.g.:
  ```js
  confidence: Math.min(this.minConfidence + bestEdge * 0.005, 0.99)
  ```
- File: `agents/bob/backend/strategies/strategies/cross_platform_arbitrage.js`
